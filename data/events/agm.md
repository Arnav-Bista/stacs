Our Annual General Meeting — come along to run for a role on next year's committee, or just to chill and vote.

**[Candidacy form →](https://forms.office.com/e/BE0sJt2Z3Y)**

You must be a member of the society to forward your candidacy. Please purchase a membership (if you haven't already) before filling out the form. If you are unable to attend in person you can run *in absentia* and have a committee member read your speech.

## Executive committee roles

- **President** — Chairing meetings; liaising with the School of Computer Science, the Students' Union, and other University organisations; running society elections.
- **Secretary** — Minutes, mailing list, membership list, website maintenance, H&S training and risk assessments.
- **Treasurer** — Financial administration of the society and its events; management of society accounts.
- **Events Coordinator** — Overall organisation of society events; supplementary risk assessments; works directly with the Sponsorship Coordinator.
- **Sponsorship Coordinator** — Liaising with current sponsors, raising society sponsorship, outreach for new opportunities; works directly with the Events Coordinator.

## General committee roles

- **Publicity Officer** — Social media, marketing, event photography, website content, society calendar.
- **CS Ball Coordinator** — Overall organisation of the Computer Science Ball, working with the Events Officer; leads the ball subcommittee and its recruitment.
- **STACS Developers President** — Leads the STACS Developers subcommittee; recruits members; organises technical workshops.

Reach out to any current committee member if you want to know more about what the jobs look like. Hope to see you there.
