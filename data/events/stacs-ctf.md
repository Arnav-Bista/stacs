# Hack the Flag! 🚩

Test your cybersecurity skills in the **STACS CTF** — designed by the STACS Dev team. Beginner-friendly challenges sit alongside harder, weirder puzzles for returning players.

## What is a CTF?

A **Capture The Flag** competition is a set of computer security challenges where you're put to the test on your knowledge of security, vulnerabilities and exploits to find hidden flags. Think treasure hunt for hackers.

## Categories

- 🔍 **Forensics** — hunt for hidden data
- ⚙️ **Engineering** — take advantage of poor programming
- 🦠 **Cyberwarfare and Malware** — real examples of real examples (harmless!)
- 🔐 **Cryptography** — the backbone of security
- 💣 **Exploit Development** — find and exploit vulnerabilities
- …and a few surprises we're not going to spoil 😉

## Format

- **Duration:** 4 hours, 13:00–17:00
- **Team size:** 1–4 people (or solo — both fine)
- **Difficulty:** beginner to advanced
- **Scoring:** points scale with challenge difficulty
- **Location:** John Honey — snacks and a live leaderboard on-site

## Prizes

A **stuffed animal** for the first-place team, plus STACS swag on the podium.

## New to CTFs?

A short beginner's workshop runs at the start of the event, and mentors from the Dev team will be on hand throughout. Just show up — you don't need a team in advance.

## Rules

1. **No attacking infrastructure** — only the challenges we provide.
2. **Collaboration within teams only** — no sharing flags between teams.
3. **Write-ups welcome** — after the competition ends.
4. **Have fun and learn.**
