Our annual 24-hour hackathon, sponsored this year by **Bending Spoons**. Teams of up to 4 people. Free food and merch for STACS members.

## Theme: Superheroes

What if you could make yourself invisible online with the click of a button? Or finish your readings with super speed? The brief is wide open — build something heroic.

## Results

Congratulations to our winners:

- **Superposition** (Team Anecdote) — computer vision × physiotherapy
  *Yehor Boiar, Soph Morgulchik, Anastasia Martinson, Fedor Lebedev*
- **Alliance** (Team SaintSave) — fighting loneliness with an AI-first platform
  *Zena More O'Ferrall, Quentin Frieser, Jessica Cutler, London Rabec*
- **SpideySense** (Team Only a Spoonful) — speech recognition for workplace equity
  *Henry Baylis, Henrikas Matusevicius, Konan Gauld, Jirakit Jarusiripipat*

Thank you to everyone who came, built, and survived the full 24 hours.
