# Connect with Top Tech Employers

The **STACS Careers Fair 2025** is St Andrews' premier tech recruitment event, bringing together students and leading technology companies.

## Featured Companies

This year's fair includes representatives from:

- **Google** - Software Engineering & Research
- **Amazon** - Cloud Computing & AWS
- **Microsoft** - Azure & AI/ML positions
- **Meta** - Full-stack development roles
- **Goldman Sachs** - Technology division
- **Palantir** - Software engineering
- **Bloomberg** - Financial technology
- And many more!

## Event Highlights

### 🎤 Keynote Speakers
Hear from industry leaders about career paths, emerging technologies, and what employers are looking for.

### 💼 Company Stands
Meet recruiters, learn about opportunities, and submit applications on the spot.

### 🎯 Workshops
- Technical interview preparation
- CV and cover letter writing
- Negotiating job offers
- Building your professional network

### 📝 CV Clinic
Get one-on-one feedback on your CV from experienced hiring managers.

## Who Should Attend?

- Students seeking **internships** (summer 2026)
- Final year students looking for **graduate roles**
- Anyone interested in **tech careers**
- Students exploring **different career paths**

## Registration

**Please register in advance** to help us plan catering and print your name badge.

### What to Bring
- Multiple copies of your CV
- Business cards (if you have them)
- Notebook for taking notes
- Professional attire recommended

---

## Important Information

### Dress Code
Business casual or smart casual recommended. Some companies will conduct informal interviews on the day.

### Preparation Tips
1. Research the attending companies
2. Prepare questions to ask recruiters
3. Update your LinkedIn profile
4. Bring a portfolio of projects (if applicable)
