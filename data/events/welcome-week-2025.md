# Welcome to STACS!

Join us for our **Welcome Week 2025** event! This is the perfect opportunity to:

- Meet fellow Computer Science students
- Learn about STACS activities and events
- Connect with the committee
- Make new friends!

## What to Expect

We'll kick off the evening with registration and welcome drinks, followed by an introduction to STACS and all the exciting things we have planned for the year. After that, we'll have social activities and games to help everyone get to know each other.

## Who Should Attend?

This event is perfect for:
- First-year CS students
- Anyone new to St Andrews
- Existing members who want to meet new people
- Anyone interested in tech and computer science!

**No registration required** - just show up and join the fun!

### Location Details

The event will be held at the **St Andrews Students' Union** main hall. Look for the STACS banners at the entrance!

---

*Free refreshments will be provided throughout the evening.*
